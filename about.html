About

⭐ Swiftie Era 🏰 Disney Adult 🐾 Animal Lover 🧠 Neurodivergent 🔬 Science Is Cool
"I don't just make things look pretty. I make them feel like you."

I'm a multi-passionate creative based in New Braunfels, Texas — autistic, curious, and fully committed to going down rabbit holes at 2am in the name of a better design.

Flutter Press Designs is where my obsessions live: brand identity, AI art, music creation, digital prints, and the endlessly fun chaos of making things from scratch.

I'm a Swiftie, a Disney Adult (that photo is proof), and an animal lover who thinks science is genuinely underrated. When I'm not designing, I'm probably making something weird with AI, watching animal videos, or listening to Taylor Swift on repeat and calling it market research.

Whether you need a logo, a custom song, or wall art for the moody corner of your living room — you've found the right weirdo for the job.

What I actually do all day

Flutter Press Designs covers more ground than most studios because I genuinely love all of it. Brand identities, event graphics, AI art commissions, custom music, canvas prints through Painted Nights on Etsy, and social media content design. Each one scratches a different creative itch and I'm not interested in picking just one.

I'm based in New Braunfels, TX and work remotely with clients everywhere. Most projects start with a conversation — tell me what you need and we'll figure out the rest.
